Once upon an evening fair,

a bohemian circus rolls into San Francisco to an iron workers' studio transformed for the evening. Food trucks arrive bringing delicacies from stone fired pizza to créme brulée while bottles are cracked merrily all around. The guests dance in circles under twinkling lights while a fiddle plays a tantalizing tune. The guests, from the Bay to London town, old and young, scientist and artist, flaunt their jolliest of dress…


One such young maiden might be found in the corner sipping a glass of champagne in a floor length dress of chiffon, chatting politely while anticipating an evening of festivites


In a classic suit, or maybe slacks and a vest, a man asks a young lady for a dance while straightening his bowtie (because bowties are cool)


On the look out for a tasty treat from the sweets trolly is a woman in a classic cocktail dress, something vogue and chic that won’t get in the way while dancing


A gentleman arrives in his traditional morning suit, ready for titillating conversation and fulfilling gaiety


As special occasions don’t come along too often, another girl, knowing this, comes to party in a gown that makes all her wildest princess-esque dreams come true


And what party would be complete without a kilt.
